Longest words in English

This the website that will tell you a lot of the longest words in English in order! This website has 2 sections normal words and place names.

    Aequeosalinocalcalinoceraceoaluminosocupreovitriolic

    52 letters

    Aequeosalinocalcalinoceraceoaluminosocupreovitriolic is the longest actual word in the english dictionary. it is a term coined by Dr. Edward Strother and is used to describe the spa waters in Bath, England which actually were formed by the Romans because it was on top of the only hot spring in England (almost the UK but Wales also has one).

    Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis

    45 letters

    Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis is the most recognised 'longest word' because not many people know about Aequeosalinocalcalinoceraceoaluminosocupreovitriolic.(I do and so do you, so don't let in on the secret). Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis is a chronic lung disease caused by inhalation of very fine silica dust.

    Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia

    36 letters

    Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia is a type of phobia that means the fear of long words. The term was coined by a group of people that wanted to create a long word that meant the fear of long words (they must have had a lot of fun).

    Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious

    34 letters

    Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious is a long word that you will probably recognise because it is famously used in Mary Poppins. The word is used to mean wonderful or good. It is debated whether it is a real word or not but I can assure you it is.

    Dichlorodiphenyltrichloroethane

    31 letters

    Dichlorodiphenyltrichloroethane is a chemical compound that is more commonly known as DDT. It is colourless, tasteless and almost odorless which was used in the Second World War to limit the spread of malaria and typhus.

    Hippopotomonstrosesquippedalian

    30 letters

    Hippopotomonstrosesquippedalian is the word used to describe if someone has Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia. From 3% to 15% of the population is Hippopotomonstrosesquippedalian. Other similar phobias are: bibliophobia (fear of books), mythophobia (fear of legends), metrophobia (fear of poetry) and logophobia (fear of words).

    Pseudopseudohypoparathyroidism

    30 letters

    Pseudopseudohypoparathyroidism more commonly known as PPHP is a rare genetic disorder in which you can get short stature, a round face, and short hand bones. PPHP can cause bone, joint, and nerve damage.

    Floccinaucinihilipilification

    29 letters

    Floccinaucinihilipilification is the action or habit of estimating something as worthless. Made in the eighteenth century, the word combines four latin prefixes flocci, nauci, nihili, pili all mean "at little value".

    Antidisestablishmentarianism

    28 letters

    Antidisestablishmentarianism is the opposition to the disestablishmentarianism of the church. It was coined in the nineteenth century in Britain and it is an unusual word because it uses double negatives.

    electroencephalographically

    27 letters

    electroencephalographically also known as EEG is a method to record the electrical activity of the brain. It is an instrument for measuring and recording the electric activity of the brain.

    Taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateaturipukakapikimaungahoronukupokaiwhenuakitanatahu

    85 letters

    Taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateaturipukakapikimaungahoronukupokaiwhenuakitanatahu is the longest place name in the world. It holds the Guinness World Record for longest place name. It is written in the language maori and literally translates to "The summit where Tamatea, the man with the big knees, the slider, climber of mountains, the land-swallower who travelled about, played his flute to his loved one."

    Llanfair­pwllgwyngyll­gogery­chwyrn­drobwll­llan­tysilio­gogo­goch

    51 letters

    Llanfair­pwllgwyngyll­gogery­chwyrn­drobwll­llan­tysilio­gogo­goch is the second longest place name in the world. It is the name of a welsh town it has 58 letters, 13 vowels and 45 consonants but would only be 38 consonants and 51 letters in the welsh language because they treat the digraphs "ch" and "ll" as single letters.

    Chargoggagoggmanchauggauggagoggchaubunagungamaugg

    45 letters

    Chargoggagoggmanchauggauggagoggchaubunagungamaugg is the longest place name in the United States and belongs to a lake in Webster, Massachusetts. The lake was named after the arrival of the English colonists and it translates to "English knifemen and Nipmuck Indians at the boundary or neutral fishing place"

    Tweebuffelsmeteenskootmorsdoodgeskietfontein

    44 letters

    Tweebuffelsmeteenskootmorsdoodgeskietfontein is a farm in the North West Province of South Africa. The name translates to "The spring where two buffaloes were cleanly killed with a single shot". Afrikaans has also made a lot of other long words, the longest (136 letters) being: Tweedehandsemotorverkoopsmannevakbondstakingsvergaderingsameroeperstoespraakskrywerspersverklaringuitreikingsmediakonferensieaankondiging

    Azpilicuetagaraycosaroyarenberecolarrea

    39 letters

    Azpilicuetagaraycosaroyarenberecolarrea was created in the Basque language the oldest language in Europe. Basque is an unusual language as it has no known relationship to any other language and nobody has been able to trace its origins. The translation of Azpilicuetagaraycosaroyarenberecolarrea into english is "The low field of high pen of Azpilkueta".

    Äteritsiputeritsipuolilautatsijänkä

    35 letters

    Äteritsiputeritsipuolilautatsijänkä is a bog in the Savukoski area of Lapland, Finland. It is in one of the most remote areas of wilderness in Finland. Nobody knows the origin or meaning of the name of the bog.

    Pekwachnamaykoskwaskwaypinwanik

    31 letters

    Pekwachnamaykoskwaskwaypinwanik is the name of a lake in the Manitoba region of Canada. The longest place name in Canada translates directly "Where the wild trout are caught by fishing with hooks".

    Venkatanarasimharajuvaripeta

    28 letters

    Venkatanarasimharajuvaripeta is a railway station in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is sometimes extended to 31 letters because the local people usually refer to it as Srivenkatanarasimharajuvaripeta. The translation of the word is "Venkatanarasimharaju's City”.

    Bovenendvankeelafsnysleegte

    27 letters

    Bovenendvankeelafsnysleegte is a farm in The Karoo, a desert region in South Africa. The Karoo is a large area that covers a section of South Africa the same size as all of Germany. It is 923 metres (3028 feet) above sea level. It in Afrikaans it literally means "Upper end of troat-cut valley".

    Mamungkukumpurangkuntjunya

    26 letters

    Mamungkukumpurangkuntjunya is a hill in Southern Australia's Antara-Sandy Bore Indigenous Protected Area. It weirdly translates to "Where the devil urinates". It is from the Aboriginal language of Pitjantjatjara.
